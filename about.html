About This Project

This website is made for a small challenge between some friends that takes place on the Rift during the 2026 ranked season.

The challenge rules are simple:

  1. Only EUW and EUNE accounts are allowed.
  2. You are not allowed to Duo with a friend during this challenge (3% of games will be allowed).
  3. You are the only one allowed to play ranked on the accounts you register (account sharing will get you disqualified).
  4. The main reason for this challenge is to have fun and for everyone to reach ranks higher than their peak.

Because not all participants are in the same rank or skill level, the leaderboard shows who has reached their peak rank and beyond.

The winner will be the person who gets further away from their peak rank.

Example:
If your peak rank is Diamond III 22 LP and you reach Master 50 LP, you will have 328 points.
If another player's peak rank was Gold IV 45 LP and they reach Emerald III 50 LP, they will have 905 points, meaning they get a higher placement.

Negative points mean you are below your peak rank.
1 point = 1 LP away in either direction.

Good luck and have fun.
